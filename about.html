our story

we've come a long way

SnailCafé started in 2016 and we've been on an epic ride ever since from our beginning as a Snail farm set up consulting firm (CFCC AFRICA) to launching the first ever all-year-round Snail meat restaurant. We've tried to stay true to our core beliefs and principles and to deliver an exceptional experience for our customers and community.
we owe a huge thank you to our community for joining us on this awesome journey, and we hope that you will continue to be part of our story

our mission

Our mission is to serve our customers great food, greet them with a smile and thank them for allowing us to serve them.

Our pledge is to establish lasting relationship with the community by exceeding expectations.
